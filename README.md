**LinkedIn Learnings – Certificates Portfolio**

This repository is a curated archive of my completed LinkedIn Learning courses, captured as certificates of completion. It reflects a multi‑year, structured effort to deepen my skills across leadership, consulting, product, data, cloud, AI, and customer-centric practices.

**Overview**

40+ certificates currently tracked as PDFs in this repo, with regular additions as I complete new courses.

Coverage from foundational soft skills to advanced technical topics, including cloud architecture, big data, machine learning, and generative AI.​

Certificates span multiple years, showing a consistent learning habit with bursts of completions in 2024–2025.

**Thematic Learning Areas**

Leadership, Management & Collaboration:

A large portion of the certificates focus on becoming a better leader, manager, and collaborator. Titles include courses on collaborative leadership, building high‑performance teams, coaching employees through difficult situations, and creating cultures of collaboration, learning, and accountability. Many certificates are also centered on communication within teams, communicating with empathy, diplomacy and tact, and business storytelling.
​
Key themes:

- Leadership mindset, collaborative leadership, and mentoring.
- Team productivity, accountability, and high‑performance culture.
- Communication foundations, storytelling, body language, and first impressions.
​

Consulting, Business & Strategy:

Another cluster builds depth in consulting and strategic thinking. These certificates cover consulting foundations (including client management, brand building, and value concepts), critical roles consultants play, and ongoing "consulting professional weekly tips." There are also courses on assessing and improving strategic plans, crafting problem and solution statements, and creating go‑to‑market and program strategies.

​
Key themes:

- Consulting foundations, client relationships, and professional branding.
- Strategic planning, business development, and value-centric thinking.
- Business collaboration and business intelligence for consultants.


Personal Effectiveness & Career Growth:

Many courses target personal productivity and mindset: building better routines, being more productive via small steps and big goals, building self‑confidence, cultivating a growth mindset and mental agility, and banishing the inner critic to unlock creativity. Others focus on career‑oriented topics such as becoming a thought leader, creating and maintaining a personal brand, and acting decisively.

​
Key themes:

- Productivity systems, habits, and routines.
- Confidence, resilience, growth mindset, and creativity.
- Thought leadership, personal branding, and career essentials.

​
Customer Experience & Service Excellence:

The certificates also emphasize creating value for customers and stakeholders. These include customer experience journey mapping, aligning customer experience with company culture, building customer loyalty, and building rapport with customers. There are multiple focused courses on customer service foundations, managing customer expectations, handling abusive customers, and serving internal customers.

​
Key themes:

- Customer experience design and journey mapping.
- Service excellence, expectation management, and rapport building.
- Internal customer focus and positive customer experiences.

​
Cloud, Data, AI & Technology:

A strong technical learning track runs across cloud, data, analytics, and AI. Certificates include multiple cloud architecture courses (core concepts, design decisions, and advanced concepts), cloud computing careers and certifications, cloud‑native twelve‑factor and fifteen‑factor applications, and beginner guides to public cloud platforms. On the data side, there are courses on big data analytics with Hadoop and Spark, analyzing big data with Hive, Apache PySpark by example, advanced SQL tuning, and data analytics for business professionals (both 2018 and 2022 editions).
​
In AI and machine learning, the repo contains certificates for applied machine learning foundations, building and deploying deep learning applications with Keras and TensorFlow, and specialized big data/ML tooling. It also includes “Career Essentials in Generative AI by Microsoft and LinkedIn,” highlighting a focus on modern AI capabilities and productivity.

​
Key themes:

- Cloud platforms (AWS, Azure, GCP) and cloud architecture patterns.
- Data engineering and analytics with Hadoop, Spark, Hive, PySpark, dbt, and SQL optimization.
- Machine learning and deep learning with Keras and TensorFlow, plus generative AI fundamentals and productivity hacks.

​
Cybersecurity & Risk:

Some certificates highlight awareness of security and risk in modern systems. These include cybersecurity foundations with a focus on application security, cybersecurity for IT professionals, and 5G technology strategy and next‑generation mobile networking. Several leadership and strategy courses also address balancing innovation and risk.


Key themes:

- Application‑level security practices for IT professionals.
- Understanding next‑generation network technologies like 5G.
- Managing innovation‑risk trade‑offs in strategic decision‑making.
